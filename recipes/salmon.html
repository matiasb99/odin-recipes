Grilled Salmon with Lemon

Description

Grilled salmon with lemon is a protein-packed, heart-healthy dish that's both rich in omega-3 fatty acids and quick to prepare. The natural flavor of the salmon shines through with just a touch of olive oil and a squeeze of lemon, making it a perfect clean-eating option that feels gourmet without the fuss.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Brush salmon with olive oil and season with salt.
  2. Grill skin-side down for ~6 minutes.
  3. Flip and cook 2-3 minutes more.
  4. Squeeze fresh lemon over before serving.
