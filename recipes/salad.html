Caprese Salad

Description

The Caprese salad is a classic Italian-inspired dish that celebrates fresh, raw ingredients. With juicy tomatoes, creamy mozzarella fragrant basil, and a drizzle of olive oil, it delivers a refreshing burst of flavor and a balance of healthy fats and antioxidants in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Slice mozzarella and tomatoes.
  2. Arrange in layers on a plate.
  3. Add fresh basil leaves.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.
